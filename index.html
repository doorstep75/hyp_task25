Welcome!

By day I am a software tester though I like to DJ in my spare time.

I am currently upskilling to become a full stack website developer so have built this site to test my developer skills and to share my DJ mixes. That's two good deeds for the price of one in my book!

There are links to my social pages on this site. If you want to keep up with my latest mixes then feel free to subscribe to any of them. I'm also active on Facebook with a dedicated page, you're welcome to follow me there too for my latest news/gossip and if you just want to say hello too.

Otherwise, I hope you enjoy some of the mixes I've shared.
